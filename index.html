My love for 'Tong Dokan'



An Architecture model of a 'Tong Dokan' collected from youtube




What is tong dokan? Tong dokan is a type of shop where you can buy tea, cigarettes, biscuits etc at a minimum cost. And the most lovable feature of this type of shop is: you can sit there for as long as you want. No one will bother you.

Usually this a type of shop maintained by a financially unstable man or unstable family. The main reason is that this type of business can be initiated with a lot less money but the income is really satisfying compared to the investment.

This type of shop is really famous place among the teens in the particular area that the 'tong' is situated. People of various age and various race comes here, take a sip in tea, smokes cigarettes and gets refreshen.
